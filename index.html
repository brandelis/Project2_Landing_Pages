Why Border Collies Stand Out

Exceptional intelligence - consistently ranked as the smartest dog breed

Athletic ability - excelling in agility, herding, and other dog sports

Loyal companionship - forming deep bonds with their family members

Trainability - quick to learn new commands and eager to please
